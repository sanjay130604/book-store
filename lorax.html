The Lorax by Dr. Seuss is a classic children’s book that delivers a powerful environmental message through a whimsical yet thought-provoking story. The book follows the Once-ler, an ambitious entrepreneur who discovers the beautiful Truffula Trees and begins cutting them down to produce his Thneeds—a product he believes everyone needs. However, his actions quickly lead to environmental destruction, and the Lorax, a small, mustached creature who "speaks for the trees," warns him of the consequences. Despite the Lorax's pleas, the Once-ler continues his business until the land is left barren, the air and water polluted, and the creatures forced to leave.
