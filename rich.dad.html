Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki is a personal finance classic that contrasts the money mindsets of two father figures—his own educated but financially struggling father ("Poor Dad") and his friend’s wealthy and business-savvy father ("Rich Dad"). The book emphasizes financial education, the importance of investing, and the mindset needed to build wealth.

Through engaging storytelling, Kiyosaki explains key financial principles, such as understanding assets and liabilities, the power of passive income, and the importance of taking risks in business and investments. He challenges conventional beliefs about money, urging readers to move beyond earning a paycheck and instead focus on acquiring income-generating assets. With practical lessons on real estate, entrepreneurship, and financial independence, Rich Dad Poor Dad serves as a guide to achieving financial freedom through smart money management.
